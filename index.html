What is it?

Do not miss updates!
Slack & Stack Overflow is a simple subscribe service that updates you about your and your co-worker's activity.
You receive Slack messages when your friend post: a question, answer, comment or when he earn a badge. Stay tuned about his activity and have fun.

How it works?

It is a simple cron job command that gets the user's Stack Overflow activity and sends a Slack message to you with the latest news about your develop friends.

Features

Except the StackOverflow feeds you can check the reputation standing between you and your co-workers. Follow the team reputation or your personal weekly and monthly progress.

What's next?

Every contributer is welcome... you can fork and help for the service improvement. Also if you have an idea don't hesitate to write us.
